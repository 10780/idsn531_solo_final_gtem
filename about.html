About Me

My name is garrett.

I have worked as a design contractor, specializing in creating innovative and functional product designs that meet user needs.



Background

I have a diverse background in industrial design, UX/UI design, web development, and graphic design. My experience spans various industries, allowing me to bring a unique perspective to each project.


Skills

  • Industrial Design: Full-stack including circuit design, firmware programming, body design, UI/UX for device interaction, and prototyping.
  • UX/UI Design: User-centered design principles to enhance user satisfaction and engagement.
  • Web Development: Creating responsive and visually appealing websites.
  • Graphic Design: Delivering compelling visual content that communicates brand messages effectively.

Tools & Technologies

  • CAD Software: SolidWorks, AutoCAD
  • Prototyping Tools: Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi
  • Web Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Design Tools: Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), Figma

My design philosophy centers around user experience and functionality, ensuring that every product not only looks great but also serves a purpose.

In my work, I am able to merge art and engineering seamlessly, creating designs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly functional.
